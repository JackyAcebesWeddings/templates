Our Story

🍁

The Beginning 2021

A hiking trail in Big Sur brought two strangers to the same scenic overlook. Clara was taking photos of wildflowers; Ethan handed her a water bottle she'd dropped without even stopping. She ran after him to say thanks. They hiked the next six miles together.

🍁

Growing Roots 2023

They planted a small garden together in their backyard in Sonoma — tomatoes, sunflowers, and rosemary. That garden became their Sunday morning ritual and the symbol they keep coming back to.

🍁

The Proposal 2025

On the last day of summer, Ethan set up a picnic in the middle of their sunflower garden. He got down on one knee between the rows. Clara said yes so fast the sunflowers barely had time to sway.
